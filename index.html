Alex Pereira

Former UFC Middleweight Champion, Current UFC Lightweight Champion

Intro

Alex Pereira is a Brazillian mixed martial artist and former kickboxer. He is the current Light Heavyweight Champion and the former Middleweight Champion in the UFC. He if only the 9th fighter to hold belts in two divisions and has attained these belts in record times (7 fights).In kickboxing, he is a former Glory middleweight and light heavyweight champion, and is the first and only fighter to have held Glory titles in two weight classes simultaneously. Pereira also competed in promotions such as It's Showtime and SUPERKOMBAT Fighting Championship in kickboxing, and for Jungle Fight and Legacy Fighting Alliance in MMA. As of 2 July 2024, he is #2 in the UFC men's pound-for-pound rankings. Pereira is the only known fighter to be a two-division world champion in MMA and kickboxing and is regarded as one of the greatest combat athletes of all time.

The Adesanya Beef

The rivalry between Alex Pereira and Israel Adesanya is one of the most intense and captivating in combat sports, rooted in their encounters across both kickboxing and MMA. Their story began in 2016 when they first met in a kickboxing ring. Pereira emerged victorious in that bout, winning by unanimous decision in a closely contested fight that many felt could have gone either way. Their paths crossed again in 2017 for a rematch, where Pereira cemented his dominance by knocking out Adesanya in the third round with a devastating left hook. This marked the only knockout loss in Adesanya’s combat sports career. As both fighters transitioned to MMA, their rivalry followed. Adesanya quickly became a star in the UFC, capturing the Middleweight Championship and solidifying his place as one of the sport’s top fighters. Pereira, who entered the UFC later, carried with him the distinction of having defeated Adesanya twice in their kickboxing days, making their eventual MMA clash highly anticipated.In November 2022, at UFC 281, Pereira and Adesanya finally faced off in the octagon for the UFC Middleweight Championship. In a dramatic fight, Pereira once again got the better of Adesanya, winning by TKO in the fifth round and claiming the middleweight title. This victory marked his third overall win against Adesanya, further fueling their rivalry. However, their saga didn’t end there. They met again in April 2023 at UFC 287 for a rematch that had the MMA world buzzing. This time, Adesanya turned the tables, delivering a spectacular knockout in the second round to regain the middleweight title and secure his first victory over Pereira. Their rivalry, defined by a mix of mutual respect and fierce competition, has become one of the most compelling stories in combat sports. With Pereira initially dominating and Adesanya eventually exacting his revenge, their encounters have captured the imagination of fans worldwide, solidifying their feud as a legendary chapter in the history of fighting sports.
